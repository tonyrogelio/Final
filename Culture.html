Freshie Walk


The Freshie Walk happened on August 16, 2024, at 1:00 PM. We started our march from Silliman Gym to the amphitheater, had a session for worship, and performed our cheer. My role was to march with the group and join in actively in cheering. This event was about inviting freshmen into the community at Silliman.

Through this activity, I got that sense of belongingness to the school. Everyone was so happy and they inspired me to be happy too. Our cheer was pretty good too.

This event strengthened my connection with my batchmates. I look forward to similar events that show school spirit. The event was beneficial to us because we got to build new connections.

Because of this activity, I am more connected to my peers and the Silliman community. I am not as shy as I started college. I now have more friends because of this.


Parada Sillimaniana


The Parada Sillimaniana was held on August 26, 2024, at 3:00 PM. We managed to march from Dumaguete City all the way to Cimafranca Ballfield where we showcased our cheer and dance. My part was to be in the parade and dance.

This event made me appreciate Sillimans culture more. It was a long walk but did not feel like one. It did not feel like a long walk because I made new friends and they were very fun.

This experience made me more motivated to be part of the university. I want to take part in the university celebrations. It was very fun and next year I would like to join.

Because of this activity, I am more proud of my schools traditions and culture. I gained new friends, very fun friends. It made me more confident.







Dal-uy Festival


The Dal-uy Festival by Foundation University took place on August 31, 2024, at 5:00 PM. I saw Foundation students floating lanterns in the water of Dumaguete boulevard. The lanterns represented hope and unity.

This event gave me a sense of peace and admiration for local traditions. It was a simple yet beautiful tradition that reminded me of the movie tangled. Witnessing the lanters float and glow on the water was magical.

This event told me to appreciate and support local cultural events. I hope to attend more beautiful events like this. This activity was very beautiful.

Because of this activity, I am more appreciative of local traditions and their symbolic beauty. Seeing a beautiful sight like that made me inspired to have my hopes up. Maybe some day I can write my dreams in a lantern and make it float on the water.






Panumduman


Panumduman, organized by Kahayag Dance Company, was held on September 21, 2024, at 7:00 PM in the Luce Auditorium. The event celebrated Kahayags 20th anniversary, featuring performances by dance troupes and guest artists. This was to honor two decades of kahayags legacy and its connection to Negros Oriental.

The event made me more appreciative for dance. I am amazed at how dance can convey stories and culture. The blend of contemporary and cultural elements made the show amazing

This event taught me to value hard work. I plan to support the workers being these artistic performances. This promoted cultural appreciation and artistic expression.

Because of this activity, I am more supportive of local art and its cultural significance. I am interested in how dances convey stories. I am amazed and would love to go to another show of Kahayag.






Kuwerdas Filipinas Symphonic Rondalla


The Kuwerdas Filipinas Symphonic Rondalla Concert Tour took place on September 14, 2024, at 3:00 PM in the Luce Auditorium. Over 50 rondalla musicians performed to celebrate 20 years of Diwang: Musika ng Rondalla, Musika ng Pagkakaisa. The event showcased the beauty of Filipino rondalla music.

This event made me understand Filipino music. I have a bigger understanding of its role in cultural unity. The performance was very dynamic.

This event inspired me to explore more about Filipino music and its traditions. I plan to attend more musical events. I am very into music and I loved this event.

Because of this activity, I am more appreciative of Filipino music and its unifying power. I am inspired to explore more traditions. I would love to watch shows like this over and over again.











CCS Acquaintance Party


The CCS Acquaintance Party, organized by the Confederates Student Council, took place on September 28, 2024, at 2:00 PM. I helped set up the stage for the event, while others managed decorations and the program. The event aimed to welcome CCS students and foster camaraderie.

This activity showed me the importance of organizing events. I realized how organizing an event impacts the audience. It was very fun and there were a lot of games.

This event taught me the value of being a working committee member. I plan to volunteer more for future activities. I suggest involving more students to help.

Because of this activity, I am more appreciative of teamwork and its impact on successful events. I am more appreciative in a way that I see the organizers efforts. I am grateful for every event.











Paulchella


The Paulchella event by St. Paul University Dumaguete occurred on October 29, 2024, at 7:00 PM. It featured food stalls and concerts and carnival rides. The event provided a fun and relaxing experience for everyone.

This event showed the joy of celebrating with friends. I really loved eating different kind of food while enjoying the music. I also rode a ride named “vikings”, a very scary ride.

This event made me more appreciative of events. This event has a role in fostering connections. Next time, I would try more food options and eat more.

Because of this activity, I am more appreciative of vibrant energy. I am more appreciative of the chefs and how they cook as fast as they can because there are a lot of costumers. I support these kinds of events and would like to join more.











CCS Christmas Party


The CCS Christmas Party, organized by the Confederates Student Council, took place on December 14, 2024, at 4:00 PM. It featured games, raffles, and festive activities to celebrate the holiday season. The event was after our exams so it felt like a relief.

This event reminded me of how joy impacts our holidays. It felt like I was back in elementary playing games. It was a fun event to end the semester.

This experience showed the value of celebrating milestones as a community. It was very fun because there were a lot of us. It was beneficial for all of us, to be more unified.

Because of this activity, I am more grateful for the joy and friendship of the CCS community. I am really thankful of this community because they have opened my eyes to the real world. This department is very fun and I hope to stay in it.
